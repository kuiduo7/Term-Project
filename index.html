About Us

Why are we building this?

Our website was created to provide more information about fitness and to awaken people to the need for fitness.

We hope that through our content presentation, more and more people will learn about the different types of fitness, learn about the different types of fitness programs and find the right fitness program for them. Thus, they will be able to do fitness better.

The purpose of our website is to help modern people to get rid of the bad health behaviors such as excessive obesity and unhealthy work and rest, and to manage their body shape better through fitness to achieve the goal of a healthy life.

Trainee profile card

As a trainee, you can show your avatar, name and basic information. Help trainer better understand your basic situation, at the same time, through personal description, you can put forward your training needs, so that trainer can better customize exclusive services for you.

Trainer profile card

As a trainer, you can share your fitness status by showing your avatar, name, the fitness category you teach and your favorite photos. At the same time you can introduce your teaching philosophy and training style in a simple text. We also offer a video upload section where you can express yourself more fully to your users and let them know more about your teaching style, which will help you find the right target group for you.

Money Plan

Each trainer can offer three different prices for its services. The first is a free basic service, where each trainer sets the content provided by the free service. The other two services are priced in ascending order, but are defined by the trainer himself, offering a richer training program and schedule based on the different prices.
